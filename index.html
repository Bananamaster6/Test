How It Works

  • Favorites beyond 100 are saved in localStorage under the key grabvr_extra_favs.
  • A floating panel overlays the page showing your full list — both site-native and locally stored.
  • Levels can be added or removed from extra favorites via ★ buttons injected next to each level card.
  • Your extra favorites persist across browser sessions (same browser, same device).
  • Works on the /levels page including ?tab=tab_newest and all other tabs.
